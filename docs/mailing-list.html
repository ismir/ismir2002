MAILING LIST

At ISMIR 2000, the music-ir mailing-list was created, in order to allow for exchanges in the topics related to this field. As of February 2002, it had well over 400 subscribers from all over the world. Announcements about the conference and updates to its Web site will also be sent to this list.

In order to subscribe, send an email to listserv@ircam.fr with the following one line in the body of the mail:

SUB music-ir YourFirstName YourLastName

 

The ISMIR 2002 Web pages will be regularly updated
to include program content and schedule
